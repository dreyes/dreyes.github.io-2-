Check out my awesome Design System

The following Design System, very creatively called "My Blog" was designed exclusively to be used along this site (my blog).

The colors

The colors are an inspiration from my two favorite colors, black and blue, and few colors to add some life.

The typography

The typography is a selection of two amazing fonts by Google Fonts, Sen and Zilla Slab. (awesome!)

The spacing and borders

The spacing is defined on an 8px basis. The borders are rounded with a 10px radius.

The components

The components used have a flat design. The elements can be round or with rounded borders.

The branding

Only two logo designs are allowed, the original logo with text and the simplistic round logo.
